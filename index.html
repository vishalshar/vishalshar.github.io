  • I am fortunate for guidance, support, and wisdom from Dr. Curtis Dyreson, Dr. Kyumin Lee, and Dr. Mengyu Wang. I am grateful to my family and friends in my journey.

Dr. Vishal Sharma did his postdoctoral training at Harvard Opthalmology AI Lab; he holds PhD and MSc in Computer Science from Utah State University and B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering from SRM Institute of Science & Technology, India. His academic research experience has focused on Compiler Construction, Data Mining & Machine Learning, and Autonomous Databases. During his tenure of about five years in the industry, he has worked/interned at Intel, Micron, and McAfee.
